Getting Started

        
      

      

        

          
1

          

            
Download the Model

            
Download the Gemma 3n E2B model file (.litertlm) to your computer. You can get it from:

            
    
              
  • Hugging Face — search for gemma-3n-E2B-it-int4-Web.litertlm
    • 
              
  • Google AI Studio model downloads
    • 
              
  • Any public CDN or object storage URL
    • 
            

          

        

        

          
2

          

            
Load the Model

            
Open the sidebar menu (☰) and either:

            
    
              
  • Paste a URL to the model file, or
    • 
              
  • Browse your local disk and select the .litertlm file
    • 
            

            
Then click Initialize Model. A progress bar will show while the model downloads and loads into GPU memory.

          

        

        

          
3

          

            
Record or Upload Audio

            
Use the record button on the main screen to capture audio from your microphone, or click the upload button (📁) to load a pre-recorded audio file.

          

        

        

          
4

          

            
Generate Notes

            
Click Generate Transcript & SOAP Note. The app will transcribe your audio and create a structured SOAP note — all locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

          

        

        

          
⚙️ Requirements

          
    
            
  • Chrome 128+ with WebGPU enabled
    • 
            
  • 8 GB+ GPU VRAM recommended
    • 
            
  • Cross-origin isolation (handled automatically by the service worker)
    •
